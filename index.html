Welcome to EliteFire Protect Ltd

Delivering professional fire protection services

We are a trusted fire protection company based in Cambridge, specialising in passive fire protection and fire stopping for commercial and construction projects.

Our services include the installation of fire stopping systems such as batt and mastic, fire-rated compounds, penetration seals, fire barriers, fire curtains, and associated compartmentation works, all in line with current regulations and manufacturer data sheets.

We focus on delivering compliant, high-quality installations that maintain fire compartmentation and meet the demands of modern construction environments.
