Our Fire Stopping Survey Services

Clear survey support for fire stopping defects and compliance requirements

Fire stopping surveys carried out to identify damaged, incomplete, missing, or non-compliant installations affecting compartmentation and fire safety performance.

Survey findings help highlight remedial requirements, support compliance planning, and provide clear reporting for corrective fire protection works.

  • Fire stopping condition surveys
  • Identification of missing or damaged fire stopping
